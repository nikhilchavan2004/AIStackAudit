# Pricing Data

## Overview

AIStackAudit uses estimated AI SaaS pricing data to calculate potential optimization opportunities for users and teams.

The pricing assumptions are based on publicly available subscription models from leading AI platforms.

---

# Platforms Considered

## ChatGPT
- Free Plan
- Plus Plan
- Team Plan
- Enterprise Plan

## Claude
- Pro Plan
- Team Usage

## GitHub Copilot
- Individual Plan
- Business Plan

## Cursor AI
- Pro Subscription

---

# Optimization Logic

The application evaluates:
- unused seats
- oversized plans
- inefficient enterprise usage
- team size mismatches
- unnecessary premium subscriptions

---

# Savings Estimation

Savings are calculated using:
- current monthly spend
- estimated optimized spend
- recommended plan adjustments

---

# Assumptions

The project uses simulated pricing assumptions for educational and prototype purposes.
